Transform MLLMs into Active Interrogators.

V-Reflection introduces a "think-then-look" visual reflection mechanism that helps MLLMs locate task-critical evidence efficiently in latent space.

Key capabilities

Think-then-look visual reflection for active interrogators

Think-then-look reflection

Reformulates visual reasoning as latent reflection, so the model can focus on task-critical evidence.

BCM: Box-Guided Compression

Spatially grounds variable-length visual regions into stable pixel-to-latent targets.

DAC: Dynamic Autoregressive Compression

Maps hidden states to dynamic probes that interrogate the global visual feature map.

End-to-end latent decoding

Inference stays fully autoregressive in latent space with efficient latent reasoning steps.

Distillation-friendly training

Two-stage pipeline (BCM -> DAC) enables stable learning and geometry-aware behavior.

Task-critical localization

Latent reasoning autonomously localizes evidence needed to answer the visual question.

Unified architecture for training & inference

Stage 1 (BCM) trains spatial compression. Stage 2 (DAC) learns dynamic probes for latent interrogation. During inference, both BCM and DAC remain inactive and the model decodes end-to-end in latent space.
